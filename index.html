About

open-fractal is a one-person studio. Every app starts from the same idea: software should be simple on the surface, deep where it counts, and quiet about everything else.

That means native SwiftUI, no analytics SDKs, no ads, and no accounts unless an app truly needs one. If an app stores your data, it stores it on your device — and you can take it with you or delete it whenever you like.
